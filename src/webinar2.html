Webinar Series: How to Build a Defensible Cybersecurity Budget

Session 1: Superforecasters

Feb 28, 2023 02:00 PM ET

This series is designed to help cyber resilient leaders evolve from focusing purely on compliance and technical controls to providing real business value from a cyber risk management lens.

Each webinar will provide access to simple tools for building a strong cyber budget that quantifies and communicates risk-driven decisions to your peers, executives, and boards. In this first session, titled "Superforecasters," participants will learn how to make accurate measurements and judgments about important events.
